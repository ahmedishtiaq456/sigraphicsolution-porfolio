Work Experience

Logo Designing (2020-2021)
In 2020, designing logos for men's brands involved creating bold and minimalistic identities, often using clean geometric shapes and a limited color palette of dark and neutral tones. Typography was typically strong and straightforward, reflecting confidence and professionalism. The focus was on simplicity and clarity, resulting in powerful logos that conveyed masculinity and modernity effectively.
Poster Designing (2021-2022)
In 2021, poster design focused on vibrant colors, bold typography, and dynamic layouts influenced by digital media. Minimalist aesthetics became popular, emphasizing clear messaging and impactful visuals, while sustainability themes gained traction. The experience centered on blending creativity with effective communication to stand out in a crowded visual landscape.
Web Designing (2022-2023)
In 2022, web design prioritized user experience with clean layouts and intuitive navigation. Dark mode became popular, paired with vibrant colors and bold typography. Mobile responsiveness was essential, while animations and micro-interactions enhanced engagement. The focus was on merging functionality with appealing design for improved user experiences.
Bussiness Card Designing (2023-2024)
In 2023, business card design focused on unique, creative formats and sustainable materials. Minimalist styles with bold typography and striking colors were popular, emphasizing brand identity. QR codes were often incorporated for easy access to digital portfolios or websites. Overall, the design aimed to make a memorable impression while reflecting professionalism and innovation.
Web Development (2024)
In 2024, web development focused on AI integration, automation, and the rise of progressive web apps (PWAs). Popular frameworks like React and Vue enhanced responsive design, while accessibility and performance optimization became essential. The goal was to create interactive, user-friendly experiences using cutting-edge technology.
